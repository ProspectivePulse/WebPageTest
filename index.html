Welcome to Water Outlook. Meter Top/Sense online portal to help you manage your water usage.

  
Water View: See how much water you are using and when you are using it.
 
Your Target: Set yourself a water saving target and track your progress.
 
Your Devices: Install a Meter Top and get real time information about your water usage.
 
Compare: Compare your consumption to the local area and city/suburb you reside in.
 
Outage: Register to receive advance notifications of a water outage.
  
Registration and access to the portal is free for homes and business in the general distribution area with Meter Top installed.
